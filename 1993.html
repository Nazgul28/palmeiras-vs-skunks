Palmeiras vs Skunks

The biggest soccer rivalry

1993 - State Tournament Finals: Palmeiras 4 x 0 Corinthians

Palmeiras smashed up the Skunks becoming the State Champion. Evair and Edmundo led the team to a huge revange after Corinthians's victory in the first game, in which Viola - skunk forward - has mimicked a pig to mock Palmeiras's fans.

Date: 06/12/1993

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Morumbi

104.401 people

Referee: Jose Aparecido

Goals: Zinho, Evair, Edislon ; Evair (OT).


Palmeiras: Sergio, Mazinho, A.Carlos, Tonhao and Roverto Carlos; Cesar Sampaio, Daniel Frasson and Zinho; Edmundo, Edilson and Evair; Coach: Wanderley Luxemburgo.


Corinthians: Ronaldo (Wilson), Leandro Silva, Marcelo, Henrique, Ricardo, Ezequiel, Marcelinho Paulista and Neto; Paulo Sergio, Viola and Adil. Coach: Nelsinho Baptista.
