1994 - Brazilian League Finals: Palmeiras 1 x 1 Corinthians

Palmeiras had no problems to win the first game and then keep the advantage on the second match to take possession of the Brazilian League trophy. Corinthians once more couldn't compete with Palmeiras of Evair, Edmundo and the rookie Rivaldo.

Date: 12/18/1994

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Pacaembu

35.217 people

Referee: Marcio Rezende de Freitas

Goals: Zinho, Evair, Edislon ; Evair (OT).


Palmeiras: Velloso, Cláudio, Antônio Carlos, Cléber, Wágner; César Sampaio, Flávio Conceição (Amaral), Zinho, Rivaldo; Edmundo (Tonhão), Evair. Coach: Wanderley Luxemburgo.


Corinthians: Ronaldo, Paulo Roberto Costa, Henrique, Gralak, Branco; Luizinho, Marcelinho Paulista, Souza (Tupãzinho); Marcelinho Carioca, Viola, Marques.Coach: Jair Pereira.
