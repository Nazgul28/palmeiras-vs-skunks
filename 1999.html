Palmeiras vs Skunks

The biggest soccer rivalry

1999 - Libertadores Cup

In 1999 Palmeiras won the most important tournament of the Continent (The Libertadores Cup) and kicked out the Skunks during the playoffs. Palmeiras's taken advantage in the first game (2x0), however the Skunks tied the series forcing the penalty-kick decision when they were defeated! "The Saint", Marcos, has appeared!!

Date: 05/12/1999 (final match)

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Morumbi

42.360 people

Referee: Oscar Roberto de Godoi


Palmeiras: Marcos, Arce, Junior Baiano, Cleber and Junior; Cesar Sampaio, Galeano (Rogerio), Zinho and Alex; Oseas(Evair) and Paulo Nunes. Coach: Luiz Felipe Scolari.


Corinthians: Mauricio, Indio, Gamarra, Nene and Silvinho; Rincon, Ricardinho, Vampeta, Marcelinho; Edilson and Fernando Baiano. Coach: Oswaldo de Oliveira.
