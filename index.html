Palmeiras vs Skunks

The biggest soccer rivalry

Palmeiras vs Corinthians is the biggest rivalry of Brazilian soccer and maybe the biggest from the entire continent. Both teams are from Sao Paulo (Brazil), the biggest city of Latin America, and both teams are centenarian.

In Sao Paulo when it's "The Derby" day the entire city stops to watch it. In those days half of the city stays "green and white" (Palmeiras's colours) and the other half is "black and white" (Corinthians's colours).
