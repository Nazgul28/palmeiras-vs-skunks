Palmeiras vs Skunks

The biggest soccer rivalry

2015 - State Tournament Semifinals: Palmeiras(6)2 x 2(5) Corinthians

The old Derby was back in town again. It was the first time ever that the Skunks were hosting a knockout game in their Stadium (Itaqueirao), but the visitors had no mercy. The match tied on the regular time and again Palmeiras beat them in the penalty-kick decison. A new "saint" was born: Prass!

Date: 04/19/2015

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Itaqueirao

Referee:

Goals for Palmeiras: V. Ramos and R. Marques


Palmeiras: Prass, Lucas, V. Ramos, Jackson and Wellington; Robinho, Gabriel Arouca, and Valdivia; Rafael Marques and Dudu. Coach: Oswaldo de Oliveira


Corinthians: Cassio, Fagner, Felipe, Gil and Fabio Santos; Ralf, Petros, Renato Augusto and Danilo, V. Love (Elias), Mendoza. Coach: Tite.
