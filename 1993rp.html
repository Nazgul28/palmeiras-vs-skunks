Palmeiras vs Skunks

The biggest soccer rivalry

1993 - Rio-Sao Paulo Cup Finals: Palmeiras 0 x 0 Corinthians

Days after the State Tournament Finals 1993, Palmeiras and Corinthians have met again in the Rio-Sao Paulo Cup Finals. It was supposed to be the skunks's revenge. However, once more the tradition has prevailed and Palmeiras was the Rio-Sao Paulo Cup Champion. Palmeiras had no problems to easily win the first match (2x0) with an outstanding exhibition of Edmundo and on the second game even though the "skunks" have tried hard the tie (0x0) was not enough. It was the second knockout game that the "skunks" have lost for Palmeiras that year.

Date: 08/07/1993

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Pacaembu

28.363 people

Referee: Marcio Rezende de Freitas

Goals: Zinho, Evair, Edislon ; Evair (OT).


Palmeiras: Sérgio; Cláudio, Tonhão, Alexandre Rosa and Roberto Carlos; César Sampaio, Amaral and Flávio Conceição (Paulo Sérgio); Edílson, Maurílio and Jean Carlo. Técnico: Vanderlei Luxemburgo.


Corinthians: Ronaldo; Luiz Carlos Winck (Leandro Silva), Marcelo Djian, Henrique and Admílson (Bobô); Ezequiel, Marcelinho Paulista and Válber; Leto, Viola and Rivaldo. Técnico: Nelsinho Baptista.
