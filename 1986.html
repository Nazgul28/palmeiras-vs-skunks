1986 - State Tournament Semi-Finals: Palmeiras 3 x 0 Corinthians

Mirandinha had his moment of glory and Corinthians was beaten in the last game of the playoffs. This game was decided on the OT and called "The Game of Justice" because in the first match Corinthians had won after clearly bad referee's interference.

Date: 08/27/1986

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Morumbi

92.982 people

Referee: Jose Assis Aragao

Goal: Mirandinha (89') ; Mirandinha (4' OT), Eder (13' OT).


Palmeiras: Martorelli, Ditinho, Márcio Alcântara, Vágner Bacharel e Diogo; Gérson Caçapa, Lino e Mendonça (Barbosa); Jorginho, Edmar (Mirandinha) e Éder Coach: Carbone.


Corinthians: Carlos, Édson Boaro, Paulo, Edvaldo e Jacenir; Wilson Mano, Biro-Biro e Casagrande; Cacau (Dicão), Lima (Ricardo) e Cristóvão Coach: Rubens Minelli.
