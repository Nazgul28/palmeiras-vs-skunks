1974 - State Tournament Finals: Palmeiras 1 x 0 Corinthians

Palmeiras, led by Ademir da Guia, one more time Champion, whereas Corinthians kept drowning on its own calvary of 20 years without a single trophy !

Date: 12/22/1974

City: Sao Paulo/SP - Brazil

Stadium: Morumbi

Referee: Dulcidio Wanderley Boschilla

Goal: Ronaldo


Palmeiras: Leao, Jair Gonçalves, Luis Pereira, Alfredo Mostarda, Zeca, Dudu, Ademir da Guia, Edu Bala, Leivinha, Ronaldo and Nei. Coach: Oswaldo Brandao.


Corinthians: Buttice, Zé Maria, Brito, Ademir, Wladimir, Tiao, Rivelino, Vaguinho, Lance, Ze Roberto (Ivan) and Adaozinho (Pita). Coach: Sylvio Pirillo.
